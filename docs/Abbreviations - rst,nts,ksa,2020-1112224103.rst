################################################################################
RST ➤ Abbreviations
################################################################################

**********************************************************************
Synopsis
**********************************************************************

Abbrevations are supported. Abbreviated words will render with an underline
and when hovered over will show a text box with their definition.

**********************************************************************
Examples
**********************************************************************

Basic
============================================================

In
    ::

        Stacks are a :abbr:`LIFO (last-in, first-out)` data structure.

Out

    Stacks are a :abbr:`LIFO (last-in, first-out)` data structure.
